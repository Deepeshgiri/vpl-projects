ABOUT

VAAG PROJECTS LLP (VPL) , an ISO 9001-2015 certified company, in the field of Infrastructure development, has wide-ranging experience and expertise in the field of providing, laying & jointing of Water, Sewage and Effluent pipelines from 100mm to 400mm diameter. These include Ductile Iron (DI) pipes, Mild Steel (MS) pipe, HDPE pipes etc.VPL has executed several such projects in the past in a time bound manner to the entire satisfaction of the clients. Several such projects are under execution.

VPL is fully equipped to efficiently carry out such kind of works anywhere in the country and has all required machinery as well as tools and plants with a complete backup of highly experienced and dedicated work-force.

VPL is expert to carry out works on turnkey basis as well. We carry out works including design and maintenance for various Government departments, NGO's as well as for private.

Further we have to our credit successful and satisfactory completion of entire water distribution networking comprising laying of more than 18 Kms. of HDPE DWC Sewage water Pipe line ranging from 250mm dia to 400mm dia pipes which was also executed in KIRARI, Delhi. This work was executed for M/S Larsen and Toubro Limited (commonly known as L&T, is an Indian multinational conglomerate company.).
